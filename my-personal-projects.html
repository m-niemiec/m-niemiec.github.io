Captcha Solving Service

Captcha Solving Service is a mock-up SaaS that allows users to send their captcha images and receive solutions in simple text format. The project is divided into 4 parts. Scraping datasets for machine learning models, GUI for renaming collected images, captcha solving OCR and captcha solving API.

Python | Scrapy | Tkinter | Keras | FastAPI | PostgreSQL | SQLAlchemy | Pytest | Docker | docker-compose | AWS | EC2
