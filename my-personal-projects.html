Captcha Solving Service

Captcha Solving Service is a mock-up SaaS that allows users to send their captcha images and receive solutions in simple text format. Project is divided into 4 parts. Scraping datasets for machine learning models, GUI for renaming collected images, captcha solving OCR and captcha solving API.

Python | Scrapy | Tkinter | Keras | FastAPI | PostgreSQL | SQLAlchemy | Pytest | Docker | docker-compose | AWS | EC2

Read Runner App

Read Runner app is meant to help people read books and all texts in general faster by showing them single words very fast in one place on the screen. Accepts pdf, epub, mobi and txt.

Python | Kivy | KivyMD | SQLite3 | Buildozer
